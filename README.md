# Europe-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard
end-to-end sales analytics project built in Power BI featuring KPI tracking, profit analysis, geographic insights, and interactive business intelligence reporting.
## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across Europe. The report enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify profitable product categories, evaluate country-level performance, and track revenue trends over time.

## Business Preview

## Business Problem

Management requires visibility into:

* Overall sales performance
* Revenue trends over time
* Most profitable product categories
* Best-performing countries
* Sales channel effectiveness
* Monthly sales patterns

## Key Findings
1. Revenue Performance
- Total revenue generated was $873 million.
- Revenue peaked around 2012 and 2015.
- Revenue experienced a noticeable decline after 2016.
2. Product Category Analysis
Top 5 revenue-generating products:
1	Office Supplies 
2	Household
3	Meat
4	Cosmetics
5	Baby Food

Office Supplies generated approximately $230 million, making it the highest revenue contributor.

3. Profit Analysis

Most profitable categories:
Top 5 Item Type	bringing most profit
1. Office Supplies $45M
2. Cosmetics	$45M
3. Baby Food	$32M
4. Household	$32M
5. Clothes	$22M

Office Supplies and Cosmetics delivered the highest profits.

4. Country Performance

Top 5 best performing countries include:
Germany
Belarus
Kosovo
Bosnia and Herzegovina
Armenia

These countries contributed significantly to overall revenue generation.

5. Sales Channel Analysis
1. 100% of recorded sales came through the Offline channel.
2. No Online sales activity was observed within the selected view.

This presents an opportunity to evaluate digital sales expansion.

## Recommendations
1. Revenue Growth
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing countries.
* Increase inventory for top-selling products such as Office Supplies and Household items.
2. Profit Optimization
* Expand sales of high-margin products including Cosmetics and Office Supplies.
* Review pricing strategies for lower-profit categories.
3. Market Expansion
* Investigate underperforming countries for growth opportunities.
* Explore new European markets with similar demographics to top-performing regions.
4. Digital Transformation
* Develop and strengthen online sales channels.
* Introduce e-commerce strategies to diversify revenue sources.
